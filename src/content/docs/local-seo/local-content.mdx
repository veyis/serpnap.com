## Local Landing Pages

Create dedicated pages for each service area or location.

```
Page Structure:
├── H1: [Service] in [City/Neighborhood]
├── Intro with local relevance
├── Services specific to area
├── Local testimonials
├── Area-specific FAQs
├── Contact info with map

Avoid:
├── Duplicate content across pages
├── Thin content (< 500 words)
├── No unique local value
├── Doorway pages
```

## Local Blog Content

```
Content Ideas:
├── Local events coverage
├── Community involvement
├── Local guides and resources
├── Area-specific tips
├── Local case studies
├── Neighborhood spotlights

Example Topics:
├── "Best [Service] Tips for [City] Homeowners"
├── "[City] Guide to [Topic]"
├── "Our Team at [Local Event]"
├── "[Neighborhood] Business Spotlight"
```

## On-Page Optimization

```
Title Tag:
├── [Service] in [City] | [Brand]
├── Keep under 60 characters
├── City name near beginning

Meta Description:
├── Include city/neighborhood
├── Highlight local benefit
├── Clear CTA
├── 150-160 characters

Content:
├── Mention city/area naturally
├── Use neighborhood names
├── Include local landmarks
├── Reference local events/news
```

> **Pro Tip:** Each location page should have unique content—not just a city name swap. Include local photos, testimonials from that area, and specific service details relevant to the location.
