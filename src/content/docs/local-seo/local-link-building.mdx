## Outreach Strategies

```
Effective Outreach:
├── Personalize every email
├── Offer value first
├── Build relationships
├── Follow up (once)
├── Be patient

Templates That Work:
├── Resource page additions
├── Broken link replacement
├── Guest posting offers
├── Expert source pitches
├── Collaboration proposals
```

> **Pro Tip:** One link from a local .gov or .edu site is worth more than 10 links from random directories. Focus on quality, relevance, and genuine community involvement for sustainable local link building.
