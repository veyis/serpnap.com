## Content Strategy

### Building Topical Authority

The #1 factor separating sites that rank #1 from those on page 2: **Topical Authority**.

```
Topical Authority = Depth × Breadth × Quality
```

**Implementation Framework:**

1. **Map Your Topic Cluster**

   ```
   Pillar Topic: "SEO"
   ├── Cluster: Technical SEO
   │   ├── Page: Site Speed Optimization
   │   ├── Page: Core Web Vitals Guide
   │   ├── Page: Schema Markup Tutorial
   │   └── Page: Mobile Optimization
   ├── Cluster: Content SEO
   │   ├── Page: Keyword Research
   │   ├── Page: Content Optimization
   │   └── Page: Featured Snippets
   └── Cluster: Link Building
       ├── Page: Outreach Strategies
       └── Page: Internal Linking
   ```

2. **Create Hub Pages** — Comprehensive pillar content (3,000+ words)
3. **Build Supporting Content** — Detailed pages for each subtopic
4. **Internal Link Strategically** — Hub ↔ Spoke linking structure

### Content Depth Framework

Top-ranking content follows a predictable depth pattern:

| Ranking | Avg. Word Count | Avg. Headings | Images | Videos |
|---------|-----------------|---------------|--------|--------|
| #1 | 2,450 | 15 | 8 | 2 |
| #2-3 | 2,100 | 12 | 5 | 1 |
| #4-10 | 1,600 | 9 | 3 | 0.5 |

**The Depth Formula:**

```typescript
// Content depth scoring
const calculateDepthScore = (content) => ({
  wordCount: content.words > 2000 ? 1 : content.words / 2000,
  headings: content.headings.length > 12 ? 1 : content.headings.length / 12,
  media: (content.images + content.videos * 2) > 10 ? 1 : (content.images + content.videos * 2) / 10,
  uniqueValue: content.originalResearch ? 1.5 : 1,
  references: content.citations > 10 ? 1 : content.citations / 10
});
```

## Link Building

Quality over quantity remains the rule. Here's what works in 2025:

### Link Quality Hierarchy

```
Tier 1: Editorial links from major publications
Tier 2: Niche-relevant authoritative sites
Tier 3: Industry blogs and resources
Tier 4: Directories and associations
```

### Effective Strategies

1. **Original Research** — Data studies earn natural links
2. **Expert Roundups** — Quotes from industry leaders
3. **Tools & Calculators** — Useful resources people link to
4. **Skyscraper 2.0** — Better + different, not just "more"
5. **Digital PR** — Newsworthy stories and data

### Link Metrics to Target

| Metric | Ideal Target |
|--------|--------------|
| Domain Rating | 40+ |
| Relevance | Same or adjacent niche |
| Traffic | Site has organic traffic |
| Link Placement | In-content, contextual |
| Anchor Text | Natural, varied |

## Entity SEO

Google increasingly understands entities (people, places, things) rather than just keywords.

### Building Entity Presence

1. **Google Knowledge Panel** — Establish your entity

   ```json
   {
     "@type": "Organization",
     "name": "serpnap",
     "sameAs": [
       "https://twitter.com/serpnap",
       "https://linkedin.com/company/serpnap",
       "https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q12345"
     ]
   }
   ```

2. **Consistent NAP** — Name, Address, Phone across web
3. **Wikipedia Presence** — Or Wikidata entry at minimum
4. **Google Business Profile** — For local entities
5. **Brand Mentions** — Unlinked mentions build entity recognition

### Entity-First Content

```markdown
Instead of: "Best SEO tools for marketers"
Write about: "Ahrefs vs SEMrush vs Moz: Comparison"

Why: Google understands these as entities and can verify claims
```
